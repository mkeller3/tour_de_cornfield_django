About Event

The Tour de Cornfield is a three-week nationwide cycling event, open to riders of any ability and any geographic location. Take in gorgeous views of corn and soybeans as you tackle countless overpasses and eat shit in gravel corners. All you need to do is register, ride your bike, and your data. Do it for training, do it for prizes, or just do it to beat your cycling buddies. Will you make it through 21 days of corn?

Tentatively planned for October 5th - 28th

The Competitions

Yellow Jersey: Mileage

The Yellow Jersey standings will be determined by total miles ridden, with mileage bonuses available throughout the race.

Green Jersey: Speed

The Green Jersey standings will be determined by cumulative average speed, with speed bonuses available for available throughout the race. This includes winning Sprint Stages by an adjusted max speed.

Red Jersey: Climbing

The Red Jersey standings will be determined by total ascent (feet climbed), with bonuses available throughout the race. This includes winning Hill Stages by climbing density of your route (ascent per mile).

About Stages

Rest Days:

  • Every Monday (3 total)
  • No riding will count towards your standings in any competition
  • Of course, you can still ride if you want to

Sprint Stages:

  • Won by adjusted max speed.
  • Adjusted Max Speed = Max Speed - (Climbing/Mile ^ 0.75) + 10
  • The adjusted max speed formula is designed to favor max speed on flat rides, so choose a route with fewer hills
  • Bonuses for Stage results:
  • Yellow: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 miles
  • Green: 0.25, 0.2, 0.15, 0.1, 0.05 mph
  • Red: N/A

Hill Stages:

  • Won by climbing intensity (ascent per mile)
  • Bonuses for Stage results:
  • Yellow: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 miles
  • Green: N/A
  • Red: 100, 80, 60, 40, 20 feet

Time Trials:

  • Won by fastest time.
  • In order for us to score stage results, you must upload a ride file of exactly the specified distance (+/- 0.5 miles). You can upload all of your files, but only the time trial file will count.
  • Routes must be out-and-back
  • Merckx-Style (road bikes only). No aero bars, aero helmets, or disc wheels.
  • Motorpacing and Group Rides are not allowed on TT-days.
  • Bonuses for Stage results:
  • Yellow: 10, 8, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 miles
  • Green: N/A
  • Red: N/A

About Rides

You can do any type of riding or racing you want during the Tour de Cornfield. Group rides, off-road rides, gran fondos, track, cross races, etc.

Rides below 13 mph are classified as trails. Mileage, average speed, and feet climbed will be multiplied by 1.8

Rides between 13-16 mph are classified as cyclocross/gravel. Mileage, average speed, and feet climbed will be multiplied by 1.5

Rides above 16 mph average are classified as road rides.

You can do multiple rides each day, and upload as many ride files as you want.

  • Races and fondos count too (Fall Fling, cyclocross, Hilly Hundred, etc.)

Climbing Density Formula

Total Feet Climbed / Total Distance


Max Adjusted Speed Formula

Max Speed-(Climbing Density^0.75)+10

Tour Prizes

Prizes will be determined at the end of the Tour De Cornfield based off of how many participants. Each competition (Yellow, Green, and Red) will have prizes for the top three.

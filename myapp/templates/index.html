Tour Rules

  • No motorpacing or group rides for TTâ€™s, Merckx style
  • Group rides and motorpacing permitted on non-TT days
  • Sprint stage wins: won by max adjusted speed
  • Hill stage wins: won by highest climbing density
  • Mondays are rest days, no riding counts (but you can still ride if you want)
  • Final stage is a short sprint stage, only 1 hour counts towards mileage
  • All rides count, including off-road (Distance and Speed is mutiplied by 1.7x)
  • Races and fondos count too (Fall Fling, cyclocross, Hilly Hundred, etc.)

Climbing Density Formula

Total Feet Climb / Total Distance


Max Adjusted Speed Formula

Max Speed-(Climbing Density*0.75)+10
